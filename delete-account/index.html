Delete Your Sokin Account

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This page explains how to request the deletion of your Sokin account and the personal data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

The fastest way is directly from the Sokin mobile app:

  1. Open the Sokin app on your Android or iOS device.
  2. Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open the Profile tab (bottom navigation, person icon).
  4. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top right.
  5. Scroll to the bottom of the settings screen.
  6. Tap Delete account (red button).
  7. Confirm the action in the dialog.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately as described below.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to:

Email: sokin.support@gmail.com

Please include the email address you used to register with Sokin so we can locate and remove your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email once completed.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your name and email address (used for authentication)
  • Your unique Sokin user ID
  • All Kundalik (mood diary) entries and mood scores
  • Your meditation play history and preferences
  • App settings stored under your account
  • Any saved progress (streak counters, favorites)

What data is retained

For legal, accounting, and abuse-prevention reasons, the following limited information may be retained for the periods indicated:

  • Subscription and billing records — retained in anonymized form for up to 7 years to comply with tax and consumer-protection law. These records do not contain your name, email, or any diary content.
  • System and security logs — automatically deleted within 90 days. These contain technical events (errors, sign-in timestamps) without diary content.

Timeline

  • In-app deletion: account is removed immediately. Backend cleanup of Firestore data is completed within 24 hours.
  • Email request: processed within 30 days from receipt.

After deletion

Once your account is deleted, you cannot recover your diary entries, history, or settings. You can always create a new Sokin account with the same email if you wish to start over.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Sokin account does not automatically cancel an active Sokin Pro subscription. To cancel a subscription, please use the Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS) settings before deleting your account. See Google's cancellation guide or Apple's cancellation guide for instructions.

Questions

For any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at sokin.support@gmail.com.
