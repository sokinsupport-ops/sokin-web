Privacy Policy for Sokin

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Thank you for using Sokin. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Sokin mobile application.

By using Sokin, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. About Sokin

Sokin is a mobile application that provides meditation content, breathing exercises, audio listening features, and personal wellness-related tools.

Contact email: sokin.support@gmail.com

2. Information We May Collect

While you use the app, we may collect the following types of information:

a) Account information

  • name or profile name
  • email address
  • Google Sign-In account information
  • user identifier

b) App usage information

  • liked or saved meditations
  • listened audio content
  • app preferences and settings
  • reminder and notification settings
  • technical activity related to app use

c) Information you provide

  • journal entries
  • mood or wellness-related entries
  • personal content you choose to save in the app

d) Device and technical information

  • device type
  • operating system
  • app version
  • language or regional technical settings

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • create and manage your account
  • provide app features and services
  • store and display meditation and audio content
  • remember your preferences and settings
  • send reminders and notifications
  • maintain app security and stability
  • improve user experience
  • identify and fix technical issues

4. Where Information May Be Stored

Some of your information may be stored or processed through third-party service providers, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Google Sign-In

These services may have their own privacy and security policies.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • with service providers necessary for app functionality
  • when required by law or legal process
  • to protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud

6. Notifications

If you enable reminders or notifications in the app, Sokin may send local or push-style notifications depending on the available implementation. You can manage notifications through your device settings or app settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • request access to your information
  • request correction of your information
  • request deletion of your information
  • request account deletion
  • contact us with privacy-related questions

To make such a request, contact us at: sokin.support@gmail.com

9. Children's Privacy

Sokin is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate legal basis or consent where required. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information, they may contact us.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

11. Third-Party Links and Services

The app may contain links to or integrations with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact:

Email: sokin.support@gmail.com
